  * Photoreceptive cells 
    - Rod cells - ~120 million, low light, monochrome vision, saturate quickly in lots of light
    - Cone cells - ~6 million, detailed, color vision, mostly packed in one region: Fovea
  * Fovea
    - small circle on the Retina 1.5mm, densely packed with cones (200,000 /mm2), highest visual acuity
    - Reading: move your eyes so the text is centered in Fovea.
